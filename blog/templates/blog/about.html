About This Blog

  • This is a blog site based on python's framework, django.

  • It has a fully functional postgreSQL database which allows the registration of new users and the creation of their profile.

  • Users are also able to login after registering and logout.

  • In case their password is forgotten they are able to reset it by providing their email address. An email is sent to them and they are prompted to follow a link in order to reset it.

  • By clicking on a user's name, other users can see all the posts he/she has uploaded.

  • Five posts per page with a working paginator on the bottom.

  • The blog's name(the-d-is-silent) is direct quote from the movie: Django Unchained.
